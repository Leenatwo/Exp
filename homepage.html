About Riyadh Metro

Riyadh Metro is your ultimate solution for fast, efficient, and safe travel across the city. It features multiple lines, connecting all major areas with state-of-the-art facilities.

Line 1: Blue Line

Route: Al Aridh to Ad Dar Al Baida

Stations: 25 stations, with interchanges at King Abdulaziz Road.)

Line 2: Red Line

Route: King Saud University to King Fahd Sports City

Stations: 14 stations, with interchanges at STC and King Abdulaziz Road.

Line 3: Orange Line

Route: Jeddah Road to Khashm Al An

Stations: 22 stations, including interchanges at Western Station and Al Malaz.

Line 4: Yellow Line

Route: Airport T1-2 to KAFD

Stations: 9 stations, with interchanges at KAFD and Ar Rabi.

Line 5: Green Line

Route: Ministry of Education to National Museum

Stations: 12 stations, including interchanges at Ministry of Education and National Museum.

Line 6: Purple Line

Route: KAFD to An Naseem

Stations: 11 stations, with interchanges at KAFD and An Naseem.
